Open-Faced Wontons

Ingredients:

Sauce

  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • tablespoon chili garlic sauce
  • tablespoon water
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 2 teaspoons rice wine vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar

Wontons

  • 1/2 cups 3-color cole slaw mix, such as Fresh Express®
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/2 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/3 cup chopped green onion
  • 25 wonton wrap, such as Mandarin®
  • 1 tablespoon chili oil, or as needed

Directions:

  1. Combine all sauce ingredients in a small bowl and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the slaw mix, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, sugar, sesame oil, and green onion.
  3. Fill each wonton wrapper with about 1 tablespoon of the filling mixture.
  4. Seal the wontons by moistening the edges with water and folding to form a triangle.
  5. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Fry the wontons in batches until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes.
  7. Drain on paper towels and serve with the prepared sauce.
